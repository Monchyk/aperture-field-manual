Somewhere in the subject's project history there is a class hierarchy built to model a note, on a page, in a book. A folder was available. It was not used. This chamber is what happens when that impulse is given a whole library instead of one side project: every miscellaneous thing that doesn't get its own chamber lives here, catalogued through the same needlessly formal lens.

Opening the Book: Charity Poker

Pick the cause you're backing. Eight players sit down, chips donated by real guests. Six Chapters play out. Whoever's chip-stack survives to the top three directs the largest share of the pot — 60/25/15 — to their chosen cause.
